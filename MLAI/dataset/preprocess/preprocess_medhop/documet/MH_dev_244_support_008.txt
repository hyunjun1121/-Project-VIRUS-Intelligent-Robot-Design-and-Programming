Cribriform-morular variant of papillary thyroid carcinoma in an 8-year-old girl : a case report with immunohistochemical and molecular testing . The description of the histological features and the immunohistochemical and molecular analyses of a case of cribriform-morular variant of papillary thyroid carcinoma in an 8-year-old girl with a family history of adenomatous polyposis is presented . The neoplasm was multifocal and bilateral , with a mixed pattern of solid , trabecular , and morular areas . The neoplasm showed angioinvasive behavior , extracapsular infiltration with extension to the perythyroidal muscles , and lymph node metastases . Tumor cells were positive for P62158 5.2 , cytokeratins 5/6 , Q15669 -1 , HBME-1 , galectin-3 , and Î²-catenin . In addition , the molecular tests did not reveal P15056 mutations , P07949 /PTC rearrangement , P25054 mutation , or P01116 mutation .
