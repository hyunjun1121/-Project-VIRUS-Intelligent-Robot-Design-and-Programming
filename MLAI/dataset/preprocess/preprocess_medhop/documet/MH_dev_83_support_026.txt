Celecoxib-induced upper gastrointestinal hemorrhage and ulceration . P35354 inhibitors are a new class of nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs with a reported benefit of less gastric and duodenal ulceration and hemorrhage . We describe a 67-year-old man taking a higher than usual dose of celecoxib ( DB00482 ) for osteoarthritis with resultant gastric erosions , ulceration , and a significant gastrointestinal ( GI ) hemorrhage .
