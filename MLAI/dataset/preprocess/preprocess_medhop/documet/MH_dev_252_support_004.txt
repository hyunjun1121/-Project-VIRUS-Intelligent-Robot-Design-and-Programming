P06213 disorders in Japan . The clinical features of insulin receptor disorders found in Japan are summarized . About 20 cases of primary receptor mutations ( type A syndrome of insulin resistance , leprechaunism and Rabson-Mendenhall syndrome ) and 16 cases of autoantibodies against insulin receptor ( type B syndrome of insulin resistance ) are described in Japan . There was a trial of DB01277 for some of the patients , revealing its usefulness .
