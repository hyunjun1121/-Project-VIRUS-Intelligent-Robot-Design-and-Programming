QUESTION: What percentage of patients of nasopharyngeal carcinoma (NPC) develop recurrent disease?

IDEAL_ANSWER:
- 1.04% of patients with nasopharyngeal carcinoma develop recurrent disease. The overall recurrence rate is 75%.
- The overall recurrence rate was 75% in HPV negative patients and 11% in HPV positive ones. Disease recurred in a spared parotid gland in three patients (1.04%).
